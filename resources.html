Additional material

The activities on this page have been inspired by the concept of intercomprehension between different languages. Here are some books to take your understanding further.

  • McCann, W. J., Klein, H. G., & Stegmann, T. D. (2003). EuroComRom–The Seven Sieves. How to Read All the Romance Languages Right Away, Aachen.
  • Hufeisen, B., & Marx, N. (2007). EuroComGerm-die sieben Siebe: germanische Sprachen lesen lernen. Aachen: Shaker.
