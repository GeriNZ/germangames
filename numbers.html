Paint by numbers

Let's practice German numbers!

Work with a partner. Use one canvas to draw a picture, connecting numbers. When you are done, dictate the numbers (in German!) to your partner. They draw what you dictate in the second canvas. Think about the best way to tell your partner the numbers, so that they can follow your directions. Compare the drawings - did you get them right?



Consider: What was difficult? What did you find easy?
